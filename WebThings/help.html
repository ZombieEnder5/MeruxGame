Controls

  • To move your camera, click and hold the Right Mouse Button while moving your mouse.
  • To equip tools, either [A] press the number key matching the tool you want to equip or [B] click the tool you want to equip. The tool number can be seen at the bottom left of the game window.
  • To unequip tools, simply equip another tool or click the tool you currently have equipped.
  • To use the tool you currently have equipped, simply click anywhere in the world and it will use it in that direction.
